📋 ABOUT OUR REVIEWS
Our reviews are scored on a scale of 1–10 across four categories: Taste, Crunch Factor, Milk Situation (how it affects the milk), and Extremeness. The final score is calculated using math that we made up. All reviews represent the official position of the Extreme Cereal Crew unless otherwise noted.

★ ALL REVIEWS — SORTED BY EXTREMENESS ★
Lucky Charms
★★★★★
9.8 / 10

The undisputed champion of marshmallow-based cereals. Yes, the oat pieces are just there to justify eating marshmallows for breakfast. No, we do not care. The color assortment of marshmallows represents genuine diversity and we respect that. Milk situation: turns lavender-pink, which is actually incredible.

✅ PROS

Marshmallows
Pink milk
Seasonal shapes

❌ CONS

Not enough marshmallows
Oat pieces are a scam
Reviewed by: Angie
EXTREMELY EXTREME
Cap'n Crunch
★★★★★
9.7 / 10

A cereal so structurally extreme that it will physically injure your mouth and you will eat more anyway. The crunch is unparalleled. The corn-and-oat flavor profile is deceptively complex. The mouth-roof situation is a feature, not a bug — it builds character. Milk situation: turns faintly gold, which is regal.

✅ PROS

Legendary crunch
Stays crunchy long
Regal gold milk

❌ CONS

Mouth injuries
Cannot be stopped
Reviewed by: Danielle
DANGEROUSLY EXTREME
Froot Loops
★★★★☆
8.9 / 10

Scientifically, all Froot Loops are the same flavor. Experientially, they are not. The red ones taste different from the green ones. This is a fact. Do not argue with me about this. The straw-sipping technique is a legitimate advanced move. Milk situation: turns slightly fruity and orange-pink. Incredible.

✅ PROS

Color variety
Straw technique
Fruit milk

❌ CONS

"All same flavor" haters
Goes soggy fast
Reviewed by: Emily
VERY EXTREME
Cocoa Puffs
★★★★★
9.4 / 10

I am literally cuckoo for Cocoa Puffs. This is not a bit. The chocolate-to-cereal ratio is perfect. They get better as they approach sogginess, which is a controversial opinion that we stand behind. The mascot has clearly lost his mind over this cereal and we relate. Milk situation: turns into hot chocolate. 10/10 no notes.

✅ PROS

Chocolate milk payoff
Good soggy
Relatable mascot

❌ CONS

Hands get chocolatey
Hard to stop eating
Reviewed by: The Crew
CUCKOO EXTREME
Frosted Flakes
★★★★☆
8.2 / 10

They're GRRREAT. Tony the Tiger said so and he was correct. The frosted corn flake is a simple concept executed at near-perfect level. Cannot be rushed — eating too fast makes you appreciate it less. Best enjoyed slowly, in the morning, while staring at nothing. Contemplative cereal. Milk situation: mildly sweet. Acceptable.

✅ PROS

Iconic mascot
Satisfying crunch
Universally liked

❌ CONS

Goes soggy FAST
Low weirdness score
Reviewed by: The Crew
GRRREAT
Honey Smacks
★★★★☆
8.5 / 10

The frog mascot (Dig'em Frog) is extremely suspicious and we have questions. Despite this, the cereal is undeniably good. Puffed wheat soaked in honey — a concept that should not work as well as it does. The sweetness level is dangerously high. Milk situation: becomes honey milk, which sounds gross and is great.

✅ PROS

Honey milk
Puff texture
Very sweet payoff

❌ CONS

Suspicious mascot
Sugar crash real
Reviewed by: The Crew
